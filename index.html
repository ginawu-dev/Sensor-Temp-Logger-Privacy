Privacy Policy for Sensor Temp Logger

Sensor Temp Logger records temperature data available from the Garmin device sensor history and stores it locally on the user's Garmin watch. The app uses this data to show daily, weekly, monthly, and six-month temperature trend summaries.

Data Collected

The app may collect and store wrist/device sensor temperature readings, timestamped temperature samples, aggregated temperature summaries, and app settings such as temperature unit, retention period, and chart display preferences.

How Data Is Used

The collected data is used only to provide temperature logging and trend analysis features inside the app.

Data Storage

Temperature samples are stored locally on the Garmin device. The app is designed to keep up to one year of source temperature samples, depending on the user's settings.

Data Sharing

Sensor Temp Logger does not sell user data. The app may send temperature summary data to a paired phone when sync or analysis features are used. If payment or trial features are enabled, KiezelPay may be used to manage licensing, trial status, and payment validation.

Third-Party Services

This app may use KiezelPay for trial and payment functionality. KiezelPay may process information needed to validate purchases and app licenses.

User Control

Users can change app settings from Garmin Connect IQ settings. Users may uninstall the app to remove app access and stop future data collection.
